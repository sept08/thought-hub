# Thought Hub 🧠

> **把自己数字化，持续自我迭代。**
> **Digitize yourself, iterate continuously.**

在 AI 横行的时代，建立个人知识管理系统已时不我待。这是我的第二大脑——一个用于目标管理、知识积累和自我迭代的数字基地。

In the age of AI, building a personal knowledge management system is urgent. This is my second brain—a digital foundation for goal management, knowledge accumulation, and self-iteration.

---

## What is this? | 这是什么？

A **PARA-based personal knowledge management system** with OKR tracking capabilities.

一个基于 **PARA 结构的个人知识管理系统**，集成 OKR 目标追踪功能。

- **Goal-oriented** | 目标驱动：使用 OKR 框架设定目标、追踪关键结果
- **Knowledge-first** | 知识优先：结构化积累笔记、资源和洞见
- **Action-focused** | 行动导向：任务管理与目标深度绑定
- **AI-ready** | AI 就绪：结构化数据为未来 AI 辅助决策打底

---

## Structure | 目录结构

```
thought-hub/
├── 0-Inbox/          # Quick capture | 快速收集
├── 1-Projects/       # Active projects & OKRs | 当前项目与目标
├── 2-Areas/          # Areas of responsibility | 责任领域
├── 3-Resources/      # Reference materials | 参考资料
├── 4-Archives/       # Completed or inactive | 归档
├── Templates/        # Note templates | 笔记模板
└── OKR Dashboard.md  # Main dashboard | 主看板
```

---

## How to use | 如何使用

1. **Daily** → Open `OKR Dashboard.md` to review tasks and goals
2. **Capture** → Quick notes go to `0-Inbox/Inbox.md`
3. **Organize** → Regularly sort inbox into Projects/Areas/Resources
4. **Review** → Update OKR progress weekly

## Philosophy | 核心理念

> 我们正站在一个分水岭：要么被 AI 淹没，要么借助 AI 放大自己。而放大的前提，是先把自己"数字化"——把经验、知识、决策模式变成可被检索、可被迭代的结构。

> We stand at a watershed: either be overwhelmed by AI, or leverage AI to amplify ourselves. The prerequisite for amplification is to first "digitize" ourselves—turning experience, knowledge, and decision patterns into searchable, iterable structures.

**This repo is that structure.**
